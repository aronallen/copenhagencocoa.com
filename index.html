Copenhagen Cocoa

Copenhagen's strongest iOS developer community

Copenhagen Cocoa is an iOS developer community that hosts a weekly informal meet-up, known internationally as NSCoder Night, where we discuss our projects and exchange ideas.

There used to be a blog on this domain but it was not maintained, so now we have a static HTML page, with our twitter feed embedded above.

Copenhagen Cocoa is still alive and kicking, you are more than welcome to drop by our mostly weekly meet-ups, details below:
